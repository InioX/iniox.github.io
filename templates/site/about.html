About Me

Hi, my name is Ini.

I am an electrical engineering student from Czechia with a strong interest in how hardware and software work.

Most of my free time is spent working with Linux, experimenting with programming languages, and building tools or small projects that help me better understand technology at a deeper level. I enjoy learning by doing, especially when it involves low-level concepts, system design, or clean, well-structured code. That's why i mainly code in Rust.

I also care a lot about design and usability. I am particularly fond of Material Design 3, and colorscheme theory in general.

I regularly work on personal and open-source projects, ranging from experiments to more polished tools. You can find most of my work, along with things I am currently learning or exploring, on my GitHub profile.
